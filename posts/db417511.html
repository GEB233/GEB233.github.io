2022-05-29- 2022-06-14

1直角坐标

1.1 各概念定义

\[ 设 f=f\left( x_{1},x_{2}\ldots\ldots x_{n} \right)\hspace{25em} \]

\[ 令F = \left( {f_{x_{1}},f_{x_{2}}\ldots\ldots f_{x_{n}}} \right)' = \left( F_{1},F_{2}\ldots\ldots F_{n} \right)'\hspace{15em} \]

则有：

\[ 梯度：\nabla f = F\hspace{25em} \]

\[ 散度：\nabla \cdot F = \nabla^{2}f = {\sum\left( F_{i} \right)_{x_{i}}} = {\sum f_{ii}}\hspace{14em} \]

\[ 旋度：\nabla \times F = \begin{pmatrix} \overset{\rightarrow}{i} & \overset{\rightarrow}{j} & \overset{\rightarrow}{k} \\ {\partial x} & {\partial y} & {\partial z} \\ f_{1} & f_{2} & f_{3} \\ \end{pmatrix}\hspace{17em} \]

1.2 几何量

\[ 线微元：\hspace{26em}\\ dl_{i} = H_{i}dr_{i}\\ 度量距离：\hspace{20em}\\ \left| {ds} \right| = \sqrt{\sum{d^{2}l_{i}}}\\ 体积微元：\hspace{25em}\\ dV = H{\prod{dr_{i}}}\\ 面积微元：\hspace{25em}\\ dA_{i} = \frac{dV}{dl_{i}} \]

1.3 平面场

\[ f=f\left( x,y\right)\hspace{26em}\\F=\left( f_{x},f_{y} \right)'=(P,Q)'\hspace{21em} \]

满足散度为零，旋度为零：

\[ \left\{ \begin{matrix} {P_{x} + Q_{y} = 0} \hspace{5em}\\\\ {\left| \begin{matrix} {\partial x} & {\partial y} \\ P & Q \\ \end{matrix} \right| = Q_{x} - P_{y} = 0} \\ \end{matrix} \right. \]

将复变函数视为平面场

\[ z = iP\left( x,y \right) + Q\left( x,y \right) \]

柯西黎曼条件为：

\[ \left\{ \begin{matrix} {P_{x} + Q_{y} = 0} \\ {P_{y} = Q_{x}} \hspace{2em}\\ \end{matrix} \right. \]
